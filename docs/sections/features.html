Features

Everything you need

No bloat. No subscription. Just a fast, beautiful launcher for your games.

🎮
Add Any Game
Browse to any .exe on your computer — Steam, Epic, GOG, itch.io, or anything else. SpiceGames handles it all.
✦
Auto Metadata
Pick an .exe and SpiceGames searches Steam automatically. Cover art, descriptions, genres, developer info — all filled in for you.
⭐
Multiple Review Sources
See Steam user review scores, Metacritic, and OpenCritic critic scores side by side. Know before you play.
🔍
Discover Page
Browse and search 50,000+ games. View screenshots, trailers, reviews, and full details before adding anything to your library.
⏱
Playtime Tracking
SpiceGames records exactly how long you play each game per session. See your stats on the Stats page.
🎨
Four Themes
Crimson, Dark, Neon, and Ember. Switch themes instantly from Settings with no restart needed.
🚀
Run on Startup
Enable one toggle and SpiceGames launches automatically when you log into Windows — ready before you are.
🔒
100% Local
No account. No sign-up. No cloud sync. Your library is stored entirely on your device. We collect nothing.
✏
Fully Editable
Every metadata field is editable before saving — name, cover, description, genres, everything. Total control over your library.
