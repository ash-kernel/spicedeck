Privacy Policy
Last updated: March 2026
Short version: SpiceDeck collects absolutely nothing. All your data stays on your device. We have no servers, no accounts, and no tracking.

1. Information We Collect

We collect no personal information whatsoever:

  • No name, email, or account details
  • No analytics or telemetry
  • No crash reports sent anywhere
  • No game library contents uploaded
  • No IP address logging on our end

2. Local Storage

All data SpiceDeck creates is stored exclusively on your device. Windows path: %APPDATA%\spicedeck\spicedeck.json. This data never leaves your machine unless you explicitly export it.

3. Third-Party API Calls

SpiceDeck fetches game metadata from public APIs when you search for a game. These requests come directly from your machine — we do not proxy or log them.
